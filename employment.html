Welcome to the MMS Trucking website. MMS Trucking was started as a truckload brokerage company in 1985 by Tom Ryan, the President of the Company. In order to provide better service to its customers, the Company started a separate trucking division in 1989. MMS was then able to pick-up, consolidate, and deliver our customers' freight in a more timely and cost efficient manner which established the Company as one of the leading Less Than Truckload carriers in Ohio and the surrounding region.

MMS Trucking is completely family owned and operated by the Ryan family. The Ryan's are dedicated to providing their customers with superior service at a reasonable cost which has been the cornerstone behind the Companys' continued success. These values are still in place today as MMS Trucking provides both brokerage and trucking services to its customers by shipping to all 48 of the continental United States.
